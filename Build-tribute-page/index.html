Hajime "Tokido" Taniguchi

Nicknamed the "Murderface" and current Echo Fox Esports player,
Tokido is considered one of the greatest fighting game players of all-time.
"Tokido crowned Evo 2017 Streetfighter 5 champion, after an unbelievable fightback from losers to defeat Punk 3-0 in the Grand Finals."

Recent Achievements:

    Premier Events:
  • Won Evolution 2017
  • 2nd Capcom Cup 2017
  • 2nd Final Round 2017
  • Won Norcal Regipnals 2018
    Major Events:
  • 5th CPT2017
  • Won The Brooklyn Beat Down Round 2
  • Won Rage 2018
  • Won Thiager Uppercut 2018
"All the fans that have been cheering for me, and everyone who played against me to defeat me and get better, egging me to defeat them and get better as well, I really want to say thank you to all of you."
Japanese quote from Tokido during the Evo 2017 Ceremony
